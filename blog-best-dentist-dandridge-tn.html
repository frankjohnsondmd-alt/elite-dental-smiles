Why Fee-for-Service Dentistry Is Different

If you have ever felt like your insurance company was quietly making decisions about your dental care, you are not imagining it. At many practices, insurance reimbursement schedules shape what gets recommended, what gets done, and what gets deferred. At our Dandridge location, we operate on a fee-for-service model, and that changes everything.

Fee-for-service means we do not accept insurance assignments or let insurance-driven minimums define your level of care. Instead, we set our own clinical standards based on what is best for you as a patient.

No compromising on materials. We use the highest-quality crowns, implants, and restorations, not whatever an insurance table covers at the lowest reimbursement tier.

No rushing. Fee-for-service appointments are not squeezed into assembly-line schedules. You get the time you need, and so does your dentist.

No hidden agendas. Our recommendations are based on your health, period. We are not incentivized to over-treat or under-treat based on what an insurance table says.

Membership plan available. Our Elite Dental Membership Plan gives patients access to exams, cleanings, and discounts on treatment, no insurance required, and no monthly surprises.
