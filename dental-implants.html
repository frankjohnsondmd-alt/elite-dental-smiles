What Are Dental Implants?

Unlike dentures or bridges, implants replace the entire tooth root and all. A small titanium post is placed in the jawbone, where it fuses and becomes a permanent anchor for a custom crown that looks and functions exactly like a natural tooth.

🦴

The Implant Post

A small titanium screw placed in the jawbone. Over 3–6 months it fuses with your bone (osseointegration), creating the strongest foundation possible.

🔩

The Abutment

A connector piece that attaches to the implant post and holds the crown in place. Custom-fitted to ensure a secure, comfortable result.

👑

The Crown

A custom porcelain crown crafted to match your natural teeth in shape, size, and color. No one will know it's not the tooth you were born with.

Your Options

Which Implant Is Right for You?

Whether you're replacing one tooth or a full arch, we have an implant solution. We'll evaluate your bone density, gum health, and goals to recommend the best fit.

🦷

Single Tooth Implant

The most common implant procedure. One implant post supports one custom crown no impact on neighboring teeth, no bone loss, no gap in your smile.

Best for: One missing tooth
🦷🦷

Multiple Teeth / Implant Bridge

Two implant posts can support a bridge spanning three or more missing teeth a fixed, permanent solution that never needs to be removed.

Best for: Several adjacent teeth
✨

All-on-4® Full Arch

Replace an entire upper or lower arch using just four strategically placed implants. A permanent, fixed alternative to full dentures done in one procedure.

Best for: Full arch replacement

Not sure which option is right for you? We'll assess your situation during a free consultation.

Step by Step

The Implant Process: What to Expect

We make the process as simple and stress-free as possible. For most patients, the entire journey takes 3–6 months from first appointment to final crown.

01

Consultation & X-rays

We evaluate your jawbone density, gum health, and overall oral health to confirm you're a candidate for implants. You'll leave with a clear plan and a transparent estimate.

1 visit · ~60 min
02

Implant Placement Surgery

The titanium post is placed directly into the jawbone under local anesthesia or under full IV sedation if you prefer. Most patients report minimal discomfort and are back to normal within a few days.

1 visit · sedation available
03

Healing & Osseointegration

Over the next 3–6 months the implant post fuses with your bone a natural process called osseointegration. This creates the strongest possible foundation for your crown.

3–6 months · healing period
04

Abutment & Crown Placement

Once healed, we attach the abutment and take impressions for your custom crown. Your final crown is crafted to precisely match your natural teeth in shape, shade, and size.

1–2 visits · final result
Are You a Candidate?

Implants May Be Right for You If…

Most healthy adults with one or more missing teeth are excellent implant candidates. Here are the situations where implants make the most sense.

😬

You Have One or More Missing Teeth

Whether from an extraction, injury, or decay implants are the most permanent, natural-feeling replacement available.

😣

You're Unhappy with Dentures

If your dentures slip, click, limit what you can eat, or simply embarrass you implants offer a fixed, permanent alternative.

🦴

You Have Good Jaw Bone Density

A healthy jawbone is required to anchor the implant. We check bone density at your consultation and can often perform bone grafts if needed.

🏥

You're in Generally Good Health

Implants are suitable for most adults. We'll review your health history and discuss any conditions that may affect healing no surprises.

🚭

You Don't Smoke (or Are Willing to Quit)

Smoking affects healing and implant success rates. We'll give you honest guidance on how your habits may impact your outcome.

💰

You Want a Permanent Solution

If you're looking for a long-term investment in your health not a temporary fix that degrades over time implants are the gold standard.
