Start Early

Why Early Dental Care Shapes Everything

The habits and experiences children have with dentistry between ages 1 and 10 set the foundation for their entire adult relationship with dental health. We take that seriously.

🦷

Catch Problems Early

Baby teeth guide the eruption of permanent teeth. Cavities, crowding, and alignment issues caught early are dramatically easier and cheaper to treat than problems discovered later.

🧠

Build Healthy Habits

Children who have positive dental experiences are far more likely to maintain good oral hygiene and keep up with regular cleanings as adults. We make dentistry feel normal — even enjoyable.

😊

Prevent Dental Anxiety

Dental fear almost always starts in childhood. A gentle, fun first experience creates confidence instead of fear — and prevents the avoidance that leads to adult dental problems.

What We Do

Comprehensive Dental Care for Kids of Every Age

👶

First Visit (Age 1–2)

We recommend a child's first visit around their first birthday or when their first tooth comes in. The goal is familiarity, not treatment — just a gentle look and a chance for parents to ask questions.

🪥

Cleanings & Exams

Regular cleanings every 6 months remove buildup kids can't get with brushing alone and let us track development, catch early cavities, and monitor eruption patterns.

🛡️

Fluoride & Sealants

Fluoride treatments strengthen enamel. Dental sealants coat the deep grooves of back teeth — the most cavity-prone areas — with a protective barrier. Simple, fast, and highly effective.

🦷

Fillings & Crowns

If a cavity develops, we restore it quickly and gently. Baby teeth with larger cavities may need crowns to protect them until the permanent tooth arrives.

😴

Sedation for Anxious Kids

For children who are extremely nervous or need more extensive work, nitrous oxide (laughing gas) and oral sedation are available. We'll never force a child through a procedure they're not ready for.

🦷

Orthodontic Screening

We monitor bite development and spacing as permanent teeth come in. Early identification of crowding or alignment issues opens the door to simpler, less expensive orthodontic intervention.

First Visit

What to Expect at Your Child's First Appointment

The first visit is low-pressure by design. Our goal is to end with a child who wants to come back — not one who dreads it.

01

Meet the Team

We introduce ourselves and let your child explore the environment at their own pace. Kids are welcome to touch the instruments, sit in the chair, and get comfortable before anything happens.

~10 min
02

A Gentle Look

We do a quick, gentle exam of the teeth, gums, jaw, and bite. For very young children, we may do a simple lap exam with the child on the parent's lap. No rushing, no forcing.

~15 min
03

Cleaning (if ready)

For children who are comfortable, we'll do a gentle cleaning and polish. If they're not ready, we never push it — that's what the next visit is for.

~20 min when ready
04

Parent Guidance

We walk you through what we found, what to watch for at home, brushing and flossing techniques for their age, and when to schedule the next visit.

~10 min
