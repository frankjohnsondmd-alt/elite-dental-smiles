What Are Porcelain Veneers?

Porcelain veneers are ultra-thin ceramic shells - about 0.5mm thick, roughly the thickness of a contact lens - custom-crafted and permanently bonded to the front surface of your teeth.

The result is a complete smile transformation with minimal tooth alteration. Each veneer is handcrafted to match the perfect shade, shape, and translucency for your face.

💎

Ultra-Thin Porcelain

Just 0.5mm thick. Natural-looking translucency that mimics real enamel.

🔧

Minimal Preparation

Gentle enamel shaping ensures a natural fit without bulky results.

💪

Built to Last

Permanent dental-grade bond. With proper care, veneers last 15+ years.
