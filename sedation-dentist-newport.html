Why Newport Patients Make the Drive

Sedation Dentistry That Actually Works

Newport and Cocke County have limited access to advanced sedation dentistry. Patients who need IV sedation or who have severe dental anxiety have historically had to drive to Knoxville. Our Dandridge office gives Newport-area patients a much closer option — 20 minutes instead of 45.

Level 1

Nitrous Oxide

You stay awake and aware but deeply relaxed. The calming effects begin within minutes and wear off completely within minutes of removing the mask — you can drive yourself home.

Best for: Mild anxiety, routine procedures, patients who want to take the edge off without deeper sedation.

Level 2

Oral Sedation

A prescription medication taken before your appointment makes you deeply drowsy. You'll be responsive but very calm — most patients have little memory of the appointment. You'll need a driver.

Best for: Moderate to severe anxiety, longer procedures, patients who want minimal awareness.

Level 3

IV Sedation

Administered directly into the bloodstream for immediate, precisely controlled depth. You'll be in a near-sleep state with virtually no awareness or memory of the procedure. Dr. Johnson is Tennessee Board of Dentistry certified for IV sedation.

Best for: Severe dental phobia, complex or lengthy procedures, patients who've failed with other sedation types.
