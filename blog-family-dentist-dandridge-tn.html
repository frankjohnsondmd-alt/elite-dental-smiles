Dental Care for Children in Dandridge, TN

We recommend bringing children in for their first dental visit around their first birthday, or within six months of their first tooth coming in. Early visits are low-key. We do a quick exam, check development, clean what is there, and spend time making sure your child feels comfortable in the chair. That comfort pays off for years.

As kids grow, we focus on prevention first. Regular cleanings, fluoride treatments, and dental sealants protect young teeth during the cavity-prone years. We also monitor jaw and bite development and flag anything that might benefit from early orthodontic evaluation.

For children who are anxious or resistant to dental visits, nitrous oxide is safe, effective, and appropriate for most pediatric patients. It takes the edge off without putting anyone to sleep, and it wears off within minutes. Many kids who arrive nervous leave saying it was not a big deal at all.

Dental Care for Teens

Teenagers tend to fall off on dental care right when it matters most. Orthodontic changes, increased sugar intake, and lax brushing habits during busy school years create conditions where cavities and gum problems develop quickly.

We see a lot of teens at Elite Dental Smiles and we keep it straightforward with them. No lectures. Just honest information about what is happening in their mouth and what they can do about it. We also offer clear aligner options for teens interested in straightening their teeth without traditional braces.

Wisdom tooth monitoring typically begins in the mid-teen years. We take periodic X-rays to track how wisdom teeth are developing and advise on the right timing for removal when it becomes necessary.

Dental Care for Adults and Seniors

Adult dental needs change over time. The core of what we do for most adult patients is preventive care: twice-yearly cleanings, exams, and X-rays to catch problems before they become expensive. When restorative work is needed, we handle it in-house as much as possible so patients do not have to be referred elsewhere.

What to Expect at Your First Visit

New patient appointments at Elite Dental Smiles include a full exam, a complete set of X-rays, and a cleaning unless the clinical exam reveals something that needs to be addressed first. We also do an oral cancer screening at every comprehensive exam.

At the end of the visit, you will have a clear picture of what is going on in your mouth, what (if anything) needs treatment, and what the timeline and cost look like. We do not rush through this conversation. If you have questions, ask them. If you want a second opinion on a treatment recommendation, we support that too.

For families scheduling multiple people in the same visit, let us know when you call. We will do our best to block consecutive time slots so everyone can be seen back to back.
