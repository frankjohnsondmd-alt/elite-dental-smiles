Are You a Candidate?

Cosmetic Dentistry May Be Right for You If…

You don't have to live with a smile that makes you self-conscious. Here are the most common situations where cosmetic dentistry makes a meaningful difference.

💀

You Avoid Smiling in Photos

If you hide your teeth in pictures or cover your mouth when you laugh, cosmetic dentistry can give you a smile you're genuinely proud to show.

☕

Your Teeth Are Stained or Yellowed

Coffee, tea, red wine, tobacco, or simply aging can darken your enamel over time. Whitening or veneers can restore a bright, youthful appearance.

💔

You Have Chips, Cracks, or Wear

Minor damage from accidents, grinding, or years of use can be repaired with bonding, veneers, or crowns often in a single visit.

↔️

You Have Gaps or Spacing Issues

Small gaps and spacing irregularities can be corrected cosmetically without braces using veneers or bonding to close spaces and even out your smile.

📈

Your Teeth Are Uneven or Mismatched

If your teeth vary in size, shape, or color, cosmetic treatment can harmonize their appearance and create a balanced, uniform smile line.

🙂

You Want to Look and Feel More Confident

A great smile has a real impact on confidence, social interactions, and even professional opportunities. Cosmetic dentistry is an investment in how you feel every day.

Common Questions

Cosmetic Dentistry FAQs

General dentistry focuses on the health and function of your teeth fillings, cleanings, root canals, extractions. Cosmetic dentistry focuses on the appearance of your smile whitening, veneers, bonding, and reshaping. Many procedures overlap: a porcelain crown, for example, is both functional (protects a damaged tooth) and cosmetic (looks like a natural tooth). At Elite Dental Smiles, we integrate both seamlessly.
Costs vary widely depending on the procedure. Teeth whitening is typically the most affordable cosmetic option, while a full smile makeover with porcelain veneers is a more significant investment. We provide detailed, transparent estimates before any work begins. Our Membership Club offers 15% off all treatment, and CareCredit financing is available with low- or no-interest payment plans.
Most cosmetic procedures are minimally invasive and very comfortable. Teeth whitening and bonding typically require no anesthesia at all. Veneer preparation uses local anesthesia so you won't feel any discomfort. For patients who experience dental anxiety, we offer nitrous oxide, oral sedation, and IV sedation so the experience is as calm as possible, regardless of the procedure.
That's exactly what your consultation is for. We evaluate your teeth, listen to your goals, and recommend the most appropriate and cost-effective options for your specific situation. Sometimes a $350 bonding procedure achieves the same result as a $1,500 veneer. Sometimes the veneer is the smarter long-term investment. We give you honest guidance never upsell so you can make the best decision for your budget and goals.
