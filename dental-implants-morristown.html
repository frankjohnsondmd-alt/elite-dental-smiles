Implant Options for Morristown-Area Patients

The right implant plan depends on how many teeth are missing, bone support, health history, budget, and whether sedation would make treatment easier.

Single Tooth Implants

Replace one missing tooth with an implant and custom crown that does not rely on neighboring teeth for support.

Implant Bridges

Replace several missing teeth with fewer implants than one implant per tooth, often with excellent strength and appearance.

Full Arch Options

For patients missing most or all teeth, All-on-4 style treatment can stabilize a full arch with a fixed or removable solution.
